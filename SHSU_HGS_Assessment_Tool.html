2 Measurement Protocol

Standard Protocol (ASHT / Southampton University)

  1. Participant sits comfortably with feet flat on the floor.
  2. Shoulder in neutral position; elbow bent at 90°.
  3. Wrist between 0–30° of dorsiflexion (neutral).
  4. Participant grips the Jamar dynamometer firmly using all four fingers; thumb opposite.
  5. Instruction: "Squeeze as hard as you can until I say stop."
  6. Maximal effort held for 3–5 seconds. No jerking.
  7. Rest ~1 minute between trials.
  8. Perform 3 trials per hand, alternating hands. Record all values below.
